Key components of my business:

  • One of the most important parts of our business model is our ability to offer custom products which the customers work with us to design pieces that fit their wants. This allows us to create lasting bonds with customers, increasing the longevity of our business.
  • One of our businesses core values is following through with customer feedback. Our smaller business model allows for more direct communication with customers and custom jewelry pieces that fit the customers wants and needs.
  • Another value we highly stress is sustainability. When we can, we choose more sustainable packaging options, often opting for paper rather than plastic.
